Our Values

At the core of FI’s vibrant culture are set of values that bring out the best in our people.

Integrity

We take responsibility and accountability for our day-to-day behavior and we hold ourselves to the highest moral and ethical standards at all times – even when under pressure. We keep our promises and set an example for others to follow

Excellence

We do this by staying curious and taking personal responsibility for our learning. We constantly look to improve our work through data and insight, and are open to new challenges and feedback because that is how we develop and improve.

Move Together

Collaboration fuels creativity and shapes ideas. We value diverse perspectives, skills, and experiences, ensuring every voice is heard. Through respect and inclusion, we create a space where everyone feels they belong.

Focused on what matters

We are dedicated to building trust in capital markets and businesses, driving sustainable change in communities and in society at large, striving to make the world a better place.

well-being

Our dedication goes beyond business; we prioritize the well-being of employees, clients, and communities, creating a lasting positive impact.

Unlock New Horizons

We push boundaries and embrace innovation to redefine possibilities. By fostering creativity and forward-thinking solutions, we empower businesses to shape a future without limitations.
